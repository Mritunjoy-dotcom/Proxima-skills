Our Mission

To make job search simple, transparent, and skills-first. We combine curated job listings, interview prep, and employer insights so candidates can focus on growth — and employers can hire with confidence.

Our Story

Proxima Skills grew from the idea that many job boards focus only on titles — not actual skills. We set out to change that by building tools that understand what a role truly requires.

"We believe the future of hiring is skills-first — it's fairer for candidates and more efficient for companies."
